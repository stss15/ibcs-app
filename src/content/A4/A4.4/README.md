# A4.4 · Ethical considerations

### A4.4.1 · Discuss ethical implications of machine learning
- Topics: accountability, fairness, bias, consent, privacy, security, environmental impact, societal impact, transparency, misinformation.

### A4.4.2 · Discuss ethics of pervasive computer technologies
- Evaluate emerging tech (quantum computing, AR/VR, AI ubiquity) and their effects on society, rights, privacy, and equity.
- Emphasise ongoing reassessment of ethical guidelines.
